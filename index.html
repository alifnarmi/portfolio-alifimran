Roast & Roll Cafe System

  • Purpose: To provide a convenient online platform for ordering food and beverages, minimizing the need for customers to wait in queues at the cafe.
  • Project Objectives:
     - Enable easy and efficient order placement from any location.
     - Enhance operational efficiency by reducing order wait times.
     - Collect and utilize customer data for continuous improvement.
  • Role: Programmer 
  • Programming language: HTML/CSS, PHP, MySQL
  • Challenge: Customer Convenience and Accessibility
  • Problem: Customers were inconvenienced by long wait times and the need to visit the cafe physically to place orders.
  • Solution: The proposed system enabled online ordering, allowing customers to place orders from their homes, offices, or on the go. This solution aimed to reduce wait times and make the ordering process more accessible.

PPAS Acknowledgment Form Portal

  • Purpose:  To simplify and streamline the process of submitting and managing acknowledgment forms for the transportation of items between business branches. By digitizing and automating this process, the website aims to reduce errors, enhance efficiency, and improve communication among departments, particularly with the Financial Department.
  • Project Objectives:
    1. Simplify Form Submission: Provide an easy-to-use, intuitive platform for staff to submit acknowledgment forms quickly and efficiently, eliminating the need for manual paperwork.
    2. Enhance Efficiency: Automate the routing and tracking of acknowledgment forms, reducing the time required to process and approve transportation requests.
    3. Minimize Errors: Reduce the risk of errors associated with manual data entry and paper-based systems by offering a reliable digital solution.
  • Individual assignment
  • Programming language: HTML/CSS, PHP, MySQL
  • Framework: Laravel
  • Challenge: Ensuring User-Friendliness for All Staff
  • Problem: Not all staff members are tech-savvy, creating an accessible user interface (UI) was crucial. A complex or confusing design could result in frustration, errors, and resistance to adopting the new system.
  • Solution: UI/UX design prioritized by conducting user research and usability testing. The website features simple navigation, clear instructions, and visual cues to guide users. Additionally, we implemented a streamlined form submission process with auto-suggestions and pre-filled fields where possible to make the user experience smooth and efficient.
